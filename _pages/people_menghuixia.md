## Menghui Xia

Ph.D. student

I am Menghui Xia, a Ph.D. candidate at Beijing Institute of Technology, beginning my doctoral studies in September 2026. My research focuses on the multiscale modeling and simulation of ferroelectric materials, spanning first-principles calculations, effective Hamiltonian methods, machine-learning interatomic potentials, and large-scale atomistic simulations. My current work explores ferroelectric switching phenomena, dielectric responses, and data-driven approaches to the discovery of novel functional materials.

Outside of research, I enjoy watching movies, making handmade crafts, and exploring new hobbies. Feel free to reach out if you would like to discuss research or shared interests.
